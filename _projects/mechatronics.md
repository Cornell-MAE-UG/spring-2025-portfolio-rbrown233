Project Overview: This is a robot that I worked on for a Mechatronics class competition. One of my main contributions was wiring everything and troubleshooting electrical components such as sensors. When attaching new sensors, I initially started by checking everything worked from the ground up, such as checking voltage on all the pins. This was slow and time consuming, though, so I switched to a different philosophy. I built the whole circuit, wired all the sensors, and the checked what didn't work. This saved a lot of time, and I think is a better philosophy for troubleshooting. Kind of like "if it ain't broke don't fix it". Here's a circuit diagram of our robot:
