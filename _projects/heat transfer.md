Project Overview: I found this problem meaningful as it shows how the governing differential equations for systems can be found, and asks the student to carry out the whole process. The initial setup involves using a physical model, in this case conservation of energy, to create a governing algebraic equation. In order to find our differential equation, we apply rate equations to replace the energy terms. We then cancel terms and are left with our final second order partial differential equation.
